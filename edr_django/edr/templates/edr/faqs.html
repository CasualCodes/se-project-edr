Frequently Asked
Questions

Find answers to common questions about AIDentifye.
If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us at ask@aidentifye.com

Is AIDentifye free?
Yes, AIDentifye is completely free to use. We believe in providing accessible and convenient eye health assessments to everyone, regardless of financial constraints.
How accurate are the assessments?
The accuracy of assessments may vary based on image quality. High-quality images yield more reliable results.
Can I use AIDentifye on any device?
Yes, AIDentifye is designed to be accessible on a wide range of devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
Can AIDentifye diagnose any types of eye disease?
While AIDentifye is capable of detecting and providing assessments of many common eye diseases, only diseases trained by the model can be identified.

You can learn more about the specific eye conditions that AIDentifye can diagnose here.
Are my personal information and images secure on AIDentifye?
Yes, we take the security and privacy of your personal information and images seriously. All data uploaded to AIDentifye is stored securely and deleted after assessment.
